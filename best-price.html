How does the best price guarantee work?

The best price guarantee is an essential part of our service promise . We guarantee that our customers will pay the best possible price for their data recovery . But what exactly does the best price guarantee mean and how does it work?

In simple terms, the Best Price Guarantee means that we guarantee you the lowest price for your data recovery. If you find a lower price for an identical data recovery service from another provider, we'll pay you the difference. We'll do our best to make you a better offer. We'll put our hands on it!

The best price guarantee applies to all our services, including data recovery from hard drives, SSDs, USB sticks, SD cards and other storage media.

How are the prices calculated?

When it comes to data recovery, there are often questions about costs. It is important to note that prices can vary greatly and depend on various factors.

To answer this question, we need to delve a little deeper into the world of data recovery. A price for data recovery can only be evaluated after our technician has examined the data carrier in the laboratory and made an evaluation

We do not calculate our prices according to the amount of data that we save, but according to the expected effort. This includes the components, the hours worked by the technicians, the equipment and the know-how of our technicians.
